# Information Architecture (Issue #3)

## Narrative objective
Communicate engineering credibility through measurable outcomes in a scroll-driven, executive-grade story.

## Section model
1. **Hero** — value proposition + 2-3 anchor metrics.
2. **Problem Landscape** — cloud cost, CI reliability, AI governance pain.
3. **Operating Philosophy** — four pillars (cost, reliability, observability, safe AI).
4. **Case Studies** — Problem -> Intervention -> Tradeoff -> Impact.
5. **AI Governance in Practice** — incident-style risk controls and reductions.
6. **Experience** — timeline with outcome-centric bullets.
7. **Web Foundry** — operator/founder positioning.
8. **Trust Layer** — logos/recommendations/credibility assets.
9. **CTA** — audience-specific conversion paths.

## Content rules
- Metrics in before->after format where possible.
- No section ships with unquantified claims unless explicitly marked directional.
- Keep narrative metaphor implicit (no comic-book language in copy).

## Mobile adaptation
- Same section order, simplified motion and denser cards.
- No long pinned scroll sections on mobile.
